Recipes Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish that has become a symbol of Italian cuisine worldwide. It consists of a round, flattened base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients (anchovies, olives, meat, etc.) baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. The result is a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed in many variations, from the classic Margherita to creative and gourmet combinations.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Olives
  • Mushrooms
  • Pepperoni or ham
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Herbs (Basil, oregano)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare or buy pizza dough and let it rise according to the recipe or package instructions.
  2. Preheat the oven to a high temperature (around 450¬∞F or 230¬∞C).
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and shape.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  5. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and your choice of toppings (tomatoes, olives, mushrooms, pepperoni, etc.).
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with herbs (basil, oregano).
  7. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve hot.
