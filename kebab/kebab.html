Recipe Kebab

Description

The kebab is a dish originating from the Middle East that has gained worldwide popularity. It consists of grilled or cooked meat (chicken, beef, lamb, or falafel as a vegetarian option), paired with fresh vegetables like lettuce, tomato, onion, and cucumber, all wrapped in pita bread or flatbread. Itâ€™s served with sauces such as garlic yogurt, tzatziki, or spicy sauce and often includes pickles. This versatile and flavorful street food is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Pita bread or flatbread
  • Meat (Chicken, beef, lamb, or falafel)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cucumber
  • Garlic yogurt sauce
  • Tzatziki sauce
  • Spicy sauce (Harissa, Sriracha)
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat of your choice (chicken, beef, lamb, or falafel) and season to taste.
  2. Grill or cook the meat until fully cooked and tender.
  3. Warm the pita bread or flatbread in a pan or oven to make it more flexible.
  4. Place the cooked meat in the center of the bread.
  5. Add fresh vegetables (lettuce, tomato, onion, cucumber) on top of the meat.
  6. Drizzle the sauces (garlic yogurt, tzatziki, spicy sauce) over the filling.
  7. Add pickles for an extra tangy flavor.
  8. Wrap the bread around the filling, making sure it's well sealed.
  9. Grill the kebab in a pan or press until the bread is golden and crispy.
  10. Remove from heat, cut in half if desired, and serve hot.
